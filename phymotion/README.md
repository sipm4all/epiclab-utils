# Phymotion commands
All commands are in the `/eu/phymotion` directory.

## home
Bring axes to their home position.

## activate
Activate axis power stages.

## deactivate
Deactivate axis power stages.

## gpos
Print position of axis stages.

## status
Print status of axis stages.

## move [x] [y]
Move axis stages to absolute position (unit: mm).

## wait
Wait until stages are not moving.
